Our Machines

STANDARD Horizontal Form Fill and Seal (HFFS) machine

These machines use heat sealable printed laminated roll stock for making filled pouches in different formats; four side sealed, bottom gusseted or stand-up. These may be with tear notches, zippers or spouts. The use of economical roll stock eliminates the need to purchase expensive preformed pouches.
